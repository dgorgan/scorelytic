    About TelosID

    Our attention to a high standard has led to extensive experience and a long history of protecting individual identities, critical facilities and infrastructure including continuous monitoring across the spectrum of business, government, and military institutions.

    Telos ID DAC

    Our aviation channeling technology provides a secure web-based solution that allows aviation entities to collect, process, and submit aviation workers' biographic and biometric fingerprint data for the purpose of security threat assessments and FBI criminal history background checks.

    Reduced costs and superior 24/7 customer care

    Flexible integration for cross-system data integrity

    Engineering for evolving TSA and industry requirements

    Enhanced functionality: scheduling tool, applicant and signatory portals

    IDVetting

    Our fingerprinting services offers organizations and individuals quick, secure, and cost-effective fingerprinting channeling services for the purpose of FBI criminal and identity history background checks. Our services make acquiring individual identity history checks easy, fast, and secure.

    Directly submits electronic biometric fingerprints

    Protect your organization from high-risk individuals

    No more messy ink cards

    Less than 24-hour turnaround for results

    Identity Infastructure

    We design, develop, test, and deploy hardware and software components that form the backbone of identity infrastructures needed for specialized requirements such as high security/integrity specifications, management of privileges, and the handling of personally identifiable information.

    Leverage biometric technologies for identity systems

    Integrate emerging cyber security technologies

    Provide state-of-the-art logistical support and maintenance

    Continuous technical support of mission critical identity systems
